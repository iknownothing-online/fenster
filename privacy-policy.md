# Privacy Policy for Fenster

**Last Updated: April 19, 2026**

Fenster ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Core Privacy Principle: Zero Data Collection
Fenster is designed with a "Privacy First" philosophy.
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
- **On-Device Processing**: All text extraction (OCR) and initial translation analysis happen locally on your device. **No screen data, images, or extracted text are ever transmitted to our servers or any third parties.**

## 2. Sensitive Permissions & Usage
To provide its core functionality, Fenster requires two sensitive permissions. We only use these permissions for the specific purposes described below:

### A. Accessibility Service (Vision Extraction)
Fenster uses the Android Accessibility Service to "read" the text currently displayed on your screen.
- **Purpose**: This is only used to facilitate text extraction for translation.
- **Usage**: The service is only active when you explicitly trigger a translation.
- **Privacy**: We do not record, store, or transmit any data accessed through the Accessibility Service.

### B. Display Over Other Apps (Optical Display)
Fenster requires the "Overlay" permission to show translated text on top of other applications.
- **Purpose**: This is only used to display the translation window (The "Optical Display") exactly where the original text was located.

## 3. Data Retention
We do not store any data extracted from your screen. Once the translation is dismissed or the app is closed, the extracted data is purged from the device’s temporary memory (RAM).

## 4. Third-Party Services
Fenster may use standard Android translation libraries. These libraries handle data according to their respective privacy policies. However, Fenster itself does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking platforms.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Fenster does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, we are inherently compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## 6. Your Rights
Since Fenster does not collect or store your data, you are already in a state of maximum privacy. There is no account to delete and no data to request.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in a new version of the app and an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
